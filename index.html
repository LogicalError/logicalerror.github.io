Non destructive, intuitive workflow

With CSG you can easily reuse pieces and block out levels.
You can go back and forth between ideas fast.

Realtime CSG allows you to drag doors, windows, pathways, large parts of
a level to new places easily while never having to think about individual
vertices or triangles. Since CSG works with solid primitives, you never
have to worry about gaps in your geometry and your physics meshes will be
created automatically.

Everything created using CSG can be easily modified and
adjusted you can keep tweaking until the very end of your project!

Changed your mind about that window your dragged into your scene?
Just press delete, done! Want a new door in that wall?
Just drag in that prefabed door that you created, that includes
the hole in that wall for you. Don't like the placement of that door?
Just move it, including the hole, in one motion!

WHY NOT MAKE LEVELS USING YOUR FAVORITE DCC TOOL?

DCC packages such as Maya or 3dsmax are great for animation and
making beautifully detailed models. When designing levels for a
game, however, your needs are very different.

You want to be able to iterate a lot and iterate rapidly!
DCC tools will have you focus on minute details when the design is not
even fleshed out! You will just end up with lots of work that you can throw out.
Realtime-CSG starts at the right end of things when it comes to game design;
you start with rough shapes and work towards more details after the
game design has settled.

If at the end you still want to use your DCC package for the tiniest of details,
then you still have the option to export your CSG model to FBX.
